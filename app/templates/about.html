About this archive

The Bayanat Document Archive is a public repository of Syrian government documents, created by the Syria Justice and Accountability Centre (SJAC).

With the opening of Syria's intelligence facility archives, this project makes selected government documents accessible to researchers, legal professionals, justice mechanisms, and the Syrian public.

All documents have been reviewed by SJAC staff and redacted where necessary to protect the identities of individuals mentioned. Documents are presented in their original Arabic alongside English translations.

This archive is powered by Bayanat, SJAC's open-source evidence management platform.
