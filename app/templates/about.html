About this archive

The Bayanat Document Archive is a pilot public repository of Syrian government documents, created by the Syria Justice and Accountability Centre (SJAC).

Inspired by Germany's Stasi Records Archive, this project makes selected documents accessible to researchers, justice mechanisms, and the Syrian public.

Documents have been reviewed, translated to English, and redacted where necessary to protect the identities of individuals mentioned.

This archive is powered by Bayanat, an open-source evidence management platform built for human rights documentation.
