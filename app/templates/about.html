About

About the Bayanat Document Archive

Built by SJAC using the Bayanat documentation platform.

The Bayanat Document Archive is a public interface for accessing selected Syrian government documents relevant to accountability, legal analysis, and historical research. It provides structured, searchable access to materials preserved through over a decade of human rights documentation work.

What This Archive Is

This archive publishes curated document records to make key materials accessible to researchers, legal practitioners, journalists, and the public. It is designed to support evidence-led investigation, enable discovery across large document sets, and contribute to long-term preservation of historically significant records.

Who Built This Archive

The archive is maintained by the Syria Justice and Accountability Centre (SJAC) , a Syrian-led human rights organization working to advance justice and accountability. Over more than a decade, SJAC has collected, verified, and analyzed large volumes of documentation related to human rights violations, supporting legal processes, policy work, and historical record-building.

This archive reflects a subset of that work, making selected materials publicly accessible while adhering to responsible documentation practices.

How Documents Are Prepared

  1. Documents are collected from verified sources, including institutional repositories and established archival processes.
  2. Each record is reviewed by SJAC staff to ensure relevance, accuracy of metadata, and suitability for publication.
  3. Sensitive personal information is redacted where necessary to reduce harm and protect individuals.
  4. Documents are structured with consistent metadata to support search, filtering, and analysis.
  5. Where available, original Arabic content is preserved alongside contextual or translated information.

How to Use the Archive

  • Use the search bar to find documents by names, institutions, locations, or keywords across the archive.
  • Browse individual records to view document details, metadata, and available formats.
  • Apply filters to narrow results by time period, entity, or classification.
  • Use document identifiers and source references when citing materials.
  • Explore connections across records to support research, investigation, or reporting.

About the Bayanat Platform

This archive is powered by Bayanat , SJAC's open-source platform for human rights documentation. Bayanat enables organizations to securely collect, organize, verify, and analyze large volumes of evidence while maintaining auditability and data integrity.
